Spinach & Artichoke Stuffed Shells
  • 1 Lb. Lg. Pasta Shells
  • 1 Box (10 oz.) Frozen Spinach, Chopped
  • 2 Cans (14 oz. each) Artichoke Hearts
  • 2 Pkgs. Cream Cheese
  • ½ Cup Parmesan Cheese, Shredded
  • ½ Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp White Pepper
  • 1 Can Tomato Sauce
  1. Preaheat oven to 350°.
  2. Blanch the shells in boiling water for about 8 minutes.
  3. Drain, then shock in cold water.
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients for the filling and stuff each shell with 2 tbsp of filling.
  5. Place shells in oven-safe serving dish, with ¼ cup of water in the bottom.
  6. Spread desired amount of tomato sauce over the top of the shells.
  7. Cover the dish with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes.

Veggie Pizza Snack
(Children's Recipe)
  • 2 Tortillas
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • ¼ tsp Dried Oregano
  • ¼ tsp Dried Basil
  • 1 Green Pepper, Sliced
  • 1 Tomato, Chopped
  • 2 tbsp Mozzarella Cheese, Shredded
  1. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. Pierce tortillas with a fork and bake on baking sheet until crisp.
  3. Remove tortillas from oven; spread eveny with tomato paste.
  4. Sprinkle with dried oregano and basil.
  5. Add tomatoes and peppers.
  6. Top with shredded cheese and bake until cheese is melted.
  7. Cut each pizza in ½ and serve immediately.

Funeral Potatoes
  • 6 Medium Potatoes
  • 1 Pt. Sour Cream
  • 2½ Cups Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  • 1 Bunch Green Onions, Chopped
  • 3 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ⅛ tsp Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Butter, Melted
  • ⅓ Cup Bread or Corn Flakes
  1. Preheat oven to 300°.
  2. In a lg. pot, boil potatoes for about 30 minutes, or until tender.
  3. Remove from heat, drain, cover, and cool.
  4. Peel and then grate with course grater.
  5. In a lg. bowl, combine potatoes, sour cream, cheese, onions, milk, salt, and pepper; mix thoroughly.
  6. Place into a greased 9x13" dish and smooth top with spatula.
  7. Combine bread or corn flakes with butter; place on top.
  8. Bake for 50 minutes, or until done.

Bacon and Cheese Nibbles
  • 20 Rhodes™ Dinner Rolls, Thawed, But Still Cold
  • ¼ Cup Butter, Melted
  • 6 Pieces Bacon, Fried and Crumbled
  • 2 Cups Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Pour butter into a 9x13" pan or 12" deep-dish pizza pan.
  3. Cut each roll into 3 pieces; roll the pieces into the butter and arrange evenly in pan.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled bacon over the rolls.
  5. Top with shredded cheese.
  6. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap.
  7. Let rise until double in size.
  8. Remove wrap; bake for about 20-25 minutes.

Crusty Garlic Twists
  • 18 Rhodes™ Dinner Rolls, Thawed to Room Temp
  • ¼ Cup Olive Oil
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, Minced
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Pepper
  • 1 Cup Fresh Parmesan Cheese, Grated, Divided
  • 2 tbsp Rosemary, Finely Chopped
  • 1 tbsp Thyme, Finely Chopped
  • Additional Oilve Oil, Cheese, and Salt (As Desired).
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Spray prep area with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Combine 9 rolls together and roll into a 10x16" rectangle.
  4. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap and let rest.
  5. Repeat with remaining rolls to form 2 separate rectangles.
  6. In a small bowl, combine olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  7. In a seprate bowl, combine ½ cup of cheese, rosemary, and thyme.
  8. Remove wrap; brush one rectangle with ½ of the olive oil mixture.
  9. Sprinkle cheese mixture over olive oil.
  10. Place second rectangle over cheese micture and press down lightly with hands.
  11. Cut along the 16" side into 18 equal pieces (less than 1" wide).
  12. Twist each one several times and place on sprayed baking sheets.
  13. Brush with remaining ½ cup cheese.
  14. Bake for about 18-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  15. Remove from oven and brush with additional olive oil, if desired.
  16. Sprinkle with additional cheese and salt, if desired.
