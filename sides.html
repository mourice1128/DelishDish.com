Spinach & Artichoke Stuffed Shells
  • 1 Lb. Lg. Pasta Shells
  • 1 Box (10 oz.) Frozen Spinach, Chopped
  • 2 Cans (14 oz. each) Artichoke Hearts
  • 2 Pkgs. Cream Cheese
  • ½ Cup Parmesan Cheese, Shredded
  • ½ Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp White Pepper
  • 1 Can Tomato Sauce
  1. Preaheat oven to 350°.
  2. Blanch the shells in boiling water for about 8 minutes.
  3. Drain, then shock in cold water.
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients for the filling and stuff each shell with 2 tbsp of filling.
  5. Place shells in oven-safe serving dish, with ¼ cup of water in the bottom.
  6. Spread desired amount of tomato sauce over the top of the shells.
  7. Cover the dish with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes.

Veggie Pizza Snack
(Children's Recipe)
  • 2 Tortillas
  • 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • ¼ tsp Dried Oregano
  • ¼ tsp Dried Basil
  • 1 Green Pepper, Sliced
  • 1 Tomato, Chopped
  • 2 tbsp Mozzarella Cheese, Shredded
  1. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. Pierce tortillas with a fork and bake on baking sheet until crisp.
  3. Remove tortillas from oven; spread eveny with tomato paste.
  4. Sprinkle with dried oregano and basil.
  5. Add tomatoes and peppers.
  6. Top with shredded cheese and bake until cheese is melted.
  7. Cut each pizza in ½ and serve immediately.

Sassy Chicken Salad
  • 1 Can Tomatoes, Diced & Drained
  • 1 Can Green Chilies, Diced & Drained
  • ½ Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 Can Chicken Breast
  • 1 Cup Celery, Thinly Sliced
  • 1 Cup Grapes, Cut Into ½
  • ⅓ Cup Toasted Pecans, Chopped
  1. In a medium bowl, combine tomatoes, mayonnaise, and honey.
  2. Stir in chicken, celery, grapes, and pecans.
  3. Cover and chill.

(Makes 4 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Powdered Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 3 Cups Orange Juice (Canned or Fresh)
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 9 Ice Cubes
  1. Blend all ingredients, except ice cubes.

** UNKOWN RECIPE **
(Makes 6-8 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Orange Juice
  • 4 Cups Iced Water
  • ¼ Cup Lemon Juice
  1. Combine 1 Cup of water (non-iced)and sugar in saucepan.
  2. Heat to boiling and boil for 3 minutes.
  3. Let cool.
  4. Add orange juice, lemon juice, and iced water.
  5. Stir and serve.
