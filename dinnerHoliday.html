Traditional Holiday Turkey
  • 1 Oven Bag, Turkey Size
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 2 Stalks Celery, Sliced
  • 1 Med. Onion, Sliced
  • 12-24 Lb. Turkey, Thawed
  • Vegetable Oil or Butter
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Shake flour in the overn bag; place into a roasting pan that is at least 2" deep.
  3. Add vegetables to oven bag.
  4. Remove the neck and giblets from turkey; rinse turkey and then pat dry.
  5. Lightly stuff with your favorite stuffing recipe, if desired.
  6. Brush turkey with vegetable oil or butter, depending on preference.
    Tip: You might use a little paprika to sprinkle on top of the turkey for a more even browning of the turkey.
  7. Place turkey in the bag, on top of the vegetables.
  8. Close the oven bag with the nylon tie; cut six ½" slits in top of the bag.
  9. Insert meat thermometer through slit in the bag into the thickest part of the inner thigh, but NOT TOUCHING BONE; Tuck the ends of the bag into the pan.
  10. Bake for:
    • 2-2½ hrs. for a 12-16 Lb. turkey
    • 2½-3 hrs. for a 16-20 Lb. turkey
    • 3-3½ hrs. for a 20-24 Lb. turkey
    • or until the meat thermometer reads 180°F.
      • Add ½ hr. for a stuffed turkey
  11. Let stand in oven bag for about 15 minutes.
    If the turkey sticks to the bag, gently loosen the bag from the turkey before opening the bag.

Holiday Ham
  • 1 Oven Bag, Turkey Size
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 12-16 Lb. Whole Ham, Bone-In & Fully Cooked
  • Whole Cloves
  1. Preheat oven to 325°.
  2. Shake flour in the overn bag; place into a roasting pan that is at least 2" deep.
  3. Leaving a thin layer of fat, trim skin and fat from ham.
  4. Lightly score the suface of the ham; insert cloves.
  5. Put ham in the bag.
  6. Close the oven bag with the nylon tie; cut six ½" slits in top of the bag.
  7. Insert meat thermometer through slit in the bag into the thickest part of the inner thigh, but NOT TOUCHING BONE; Tuck the ends of the bag into the pan.
  8. Bake for 2½-3¼ hrs. or until meat thermometer reads 140°F.
  9. Let stand in oven bag for about 15 minutes.
