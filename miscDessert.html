RaeShel's Horton Family S'More Treats
  • 16 oz Bag Marshmallows
  • 12 oz Chocolate Chips
  • 8 oz Bag Marshmallows
  • ⅓ Cup Karo® Dark Syrup
  • 5 tbsp Butter
  • 12 oz Golden Graham® Cereal
  1. In large bowl, combine 16 oz bag of marshmallows, chocolate chips, syrup, and butter
  2. Microwave in 1 minutes intervals, until melted; stiring after each interval
  3. In a seperate large bowl, combine Cereal and 8 oz bag of marshmallows
  4. Pour liquid mixture over cereal mix; continue to mix until well blended
  5. Enjoy!

German Chocolate Pizza
  • 6 Rhodes Texas™ Rolls or 9 Rhodes™ Dinner Rolls, Thawed
  • 1 Cup Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Cup Shredded Coconut
  • 1 Cup Chopped Pecans
  • 1 Can (14 oz.) Sweetened Condensed Milk
  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Combine rolls into ball, then roll into a 13" circle.
  3. Place dough on a slightly smaller 12" srayed pizza pan.
  4. Turn up the edges of the dough to form a ridge.
  5. Poke several times with a fork to prevent bubbles from forming.
  6. Bake for 5 minutes at 400° for 5 minutes.
  7. Layer pre-baked crust with chocolate chips, coconut, and pecans.
  8. Drizzle with condensed milk and bake at 350° for 15-20 minutes.

Better Than Sex (Yeah Right! LOL)
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • ½ Cup Butter, Melted
  • 1 pkg. Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup Powdered Sugar
  • 2 Tubs (9 oz. each) Whipped Topping
  • 3 Cups Milk
  • 2 Pkgs. Pudding (Chocolate or Butterscotch)
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In mixing bowl, combine the sugar, flour, and melted butter.
  3. Press into a 9x13" baking dish.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, and 1 tub of the whipped cream.
  6. Spread over the top of the 1st layer.
  7. Combine milk and pudding mix in saucepan and cook until mixed well; then pour over the 2nd layer.
  8. Layer the remaining tub of whipped cream on top.
  9. Cool in fridge for several hours before serving.

Quick Cobbler
  • ¼ Butter (½ Stick)
  • ⅔ Cup Sugar (Use a little more sugar if using fresh fruit)
  • 1 Cup Bisquick®
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 Cans Fruit Pie Filling or
  • 2 Cups Fresh Fruit (Whatever kind desired)
  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Melt the butter in 13x9" baking dish.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the sugar, Bisquick®, milk, and salt.
  4. Pour batter into baking dish with the melted butter.
  5. Add the 2 cans of fruit pie filling.
    6. or
  6. Instead of using the pie filling, you could use 2 cups of fresh fruit.
  7. The batter will bubble up around the fruit; no need to mix it around too much, just enough to distribute.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes.

Peach Cobbler Supreme
  • ¼ Butter (½ Stick)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Can (29 oz) Sliced Peaches
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon
  • ⅔ Cup Milk
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Flour (Sifted, if you can)
  • ¼ tsp Nutmeg
  • ½ tsp Lemon Peel
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Melt the butter in 7x11" baking dish.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the dry ingredients.
  4. Add milk, stirring well.
  5. Pour mixture into the baking dish. (DO NOT STIR)
  6. Top with peaches, including juice.
  7. Sprinkle nutmeg, cinnamon, and lemon peel over peaches. (DO NOT STIR)
  8. Bake for 40 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Serve warm with cream.
