Pasta Salad

Welcome to the pasta salad section!

Please scroll down the page to see the delicious pasta salad ideas.

Rainbow Rotini Salad
  • 16 oz. Colored Rotini Pasta
  • 2 Tomatoes, Chopped
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper, Chopped
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Lg Cucumber, Chopped
  • 1 Cup Broccoli Florets
  • 1 Cup Mushrooms, Sliced (Optional)
  • 1 Bottle (8 oz.) Italian Salad Dressing
  • 1 Cup Black Olives, Pitted & Sliced
  1. In large pan, bring 4 qts. of water to rapid boil; add rotini.
  2. Return water to a rapid boil & cook uncovered, stirring frequently for about 10-12 minutes.
  3. Drain & rinse in cold water.
  4. Combine cooked pasta with all other ingredients.
  5. Stir, cover, and chill.

Herb & Tomato White Cheddar Pasta w/Vegetables
  • 1 Pkg. (6.2 oz.) Pasta Roni® Shells & White Cheddar
  • ¾ Cup Grape or Cherry Tomatoes, Cut Into ½
  • ½ Cup Corn, Fresh or Frozen
  • ¼ Cup Peas, Frozen
  • ¼ Cup Black Olives, Pitted & Sliced (Optional)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Basil Leaves, Thinnly Sliced
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)
  1. Prepare Pasta Roni® according to the package instructions.
  2. Stir tomatoes, corn, peas, and olives into pasta mixture.
  3. Stir in basil; garnish with red pepper flakes.

Baked Ziti Casserole
  • Cooking Spray
  • 8 oz. Zitti Pasta, Dry
  • 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 Can (26 oz.) Pasta Sauce
  • 1 Can (6 oz.) Tomato Paste
  • 1 Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°.
  2. Spray 8x8" baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Cook pasta per package directions; drain.
  4. Cook beef in a Lg. Skillet over medium-high heat for 7 minutes or until no longer pink, sirring occasionally; drain.
  5. Stir in pasta sauce and tomato paste until combined.
  6. Spoon ½ cup of the meat sauce into bottom of prepared baking dish.
  7. Top with ½ of the pasta, then ½ of the remaining meat sauce, and then ½ cup of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, beinning with meat and ending with cheese.
  9. Bake, uncovered, for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted; serve immediately.
