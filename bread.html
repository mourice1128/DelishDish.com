Braided Athenian Bread
  • 2 Loaf Rhodes™ Bread Dough
    or
    12 Rhodes™ Dinner Rolls, Thawed
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Can (6 oz.) Whole Black Olives, sliced
  • ¼ Cup Italian Parsley, Chopped
  • 2 tbsp Capers, Drained
  • ¼ Cup Tomatoes, Sliced
  • ¼ Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • ¼ Parmesan Cheese, Grated
  • 1 Egg, Beaten
  • Poppy Seeds
  1. Spray prep area with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Roll loaf or combined dinner rolls into a 12x16" rectangle.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest for about 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine garlic, olives, parsley, capers, tomatoes, sour cream, and black pepper.
  5. Remove the plastic wrap from the dough and spread filling lengthwise in a 4" strip down the center of the dough.
  6. Sprinkle filling with parmesan cheese.
  7. Make cuts 1½" apart on long sides of dough to within ½" of filling.
  8. Begin braid by folding top and bottom strips toward filling.
  9. Then braid strips left over right, right over left.
  10. Finish by pulling last strip over and tucking under braid.
  11. Lift braidwith both hands and place on lg. sprayed baking sheet.
  12. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
  13. Cover with sprayed plastic wrap and let rise for about 30 minutes.
  14. Remove wrap and bake at 350° for about 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  15. Cool slightly and slice to serve.

Zucchini Bread
(Makes 2 Loaves)
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Cups Zucchini, Peeled, Grated
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 3 tsp Cinnamon
  • ½ Cup Nuts, Chopped
  • 3 Cups Flour
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° if using metal pans; 325° if using glass pans.
  2. Beat eggs, add wet ingredients, then dry ingredients.
  3. Beat well, add nuts, and then divide batter into 2 well-greased and floured loaf pans.
  4. Bake at 350° for 60-70 minutes in metal pans or 325° for 60 minutes in glass pans.
