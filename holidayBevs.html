Eggnog
(Makes 1 Serving)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • ¼ tsp Vanilla
  • Dash of Nutmeg (Optional)
  1. Beat all ingrediensts (except nutmeg) with beater.
  2. Pour into glass; sprinkle lightly with nutmeg (Optional).

Fruit Eggnog
(Makes 1 Serving)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Tbsp Fruit (Peaches, Apricots, Berries, ect.), Blended
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Dash of Nutmeg (Optional)
  1. Beat all ingrediensts (except nutmeg) with beater.
  2. Pour into glass; sprinkle lightly with nutmeg (Optional).

Chocolate Eggnog
(Makes 1 Serving)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • ¼ Cup Chocolate Mix
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • ¼ tsp Vanilla
  • Dash of Nutmeg (Optional)
  1. Beat all ingrediensts (except nutmeg) with beater.
  2. Pour into glass; sprinkle lightly with nutmeg (Optional).

Sparkling Holiday Punch
(Makes 30 Servings)
  • 3 Cups Cold Water
  • ¾ Cup Sugar
  • 5 Cinnamon Sticks
  • 1 Bottle Sparkling Fruit Juice Cocktail (Any Flavor), Chilled
  • 1 Cup Orange Juice
  • ½ Cup Lemon Juice
  • 3 Bottles (16 Oz. Each) Lemon-Lime Soda, Chilled
  • Ice Cubes Made w/ Fruit Juice Cocktail
  1. In a medium sausepan, combine water, sugar, and cinnamon sticks to make a cinnamon syrup.
  2. Heat to boiling over medium-heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove cinnamon sticks, cover and refridgerate for 2 hours.
  5. In a Lg. punch bowl, combine cinnamon syrup, sparkling cocktail, orange and lemon juice, and soda; add ice cubes.
  6. If desired, garnish with additional cinnamon sticks, fruit-filled ice cubes, or fruit slices.
