Super Meat Sauce
  • 4 Slices Bacon, Diced
  • 3 oz. Butter or Olive Oil
  • 1 Medium-Sized Red Onion, Chopped
  • 5 Fresh Mushrooms, Chopped
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Chpped
  • 8 oz. Ground Pork
  • 8 oz. Ground Chuck
  • 8 oz. Sprite®
  • 1 Can (½ Lb.) Tomato Purée
  • 1 can (4 oz.) Tomato Paste
  • ½ Gal. Whole Tomatoes
  • 1 qt. Chicken or Beef Stock
  • ½ Cup Parsley, Chopped
  • 1 Stock Celery, Chopped
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • Pinch of Thyme
  • Pinch of Rosemary
  • Pinch of Oregano
  • Pinch of Nutmeg
  • 1 Clove
  • Few Leaves of Basil or Salt and Pepper or Parsely, to Garnish
  1. In a saucepan, cook the bacon.
  2. Add the butter or oil and the onions.
  3. Once lightly browned, add the mushrooms and garlic.
  4. Add pork and beef.
  5. After a few minutes, add the Sprite® and then the rest of the spices (except parsley), tomatoes, and liquids.
  6. Cook slowly for about 2 hours.
  7. Before serving, remove the clove and add the chopped parsley.
  8. Garnish with additional parsley.

Orange Sauce
(For Ham, Chicken, or Turkey)
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 Cup Minute Maid® Orange Juice
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ Cup Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Orange Peel, Grated
  • 1 tsp Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Orange, Thinly Sliced
  • Additional Grated Peel for Garnish
  1. Mix the 1st 7 ingredients and cook over low heat until clear.
  2. Pour sauce over meat.
  3. Garnish with additional orange slices and/or additional grated peel.

Meat Marinade and Gravy
  • 1 Cup Cola
  • 1 Cup Cooking Oil
  • 1 tbsp Steak Sauce
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Crushed
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Can Mushrooms
  1. Combine all ingredents.
  2. Marinate meat at room temp for 2 hours prior to cooking.
    3. ► You can use this marinade to baste a roast - very often. ◄

Super BBQ Sauce
  • 1 Cup Cola
  • 1¼ Cups Ketchup
  • ¼ Lb. Butter
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 2 Cups Worcestershire Sauce
  • 4 tsp Sugar
  • 1-2 tbsp Ground Black Pepper
  1. Combine all ingredents in a heavy pot.
  2. Bring to boil over low heat.
  3. Cook, stirring frequently, for about 30 minutes.
  4. Brush on food as it is grilled.
