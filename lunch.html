Baked Turkey and Cheese Sandwiches
  • 1 tbsp Butter, Softened
  • 8 Slices of Bread (Depending on taste desired)
  • 8 Slices of Deli Turkey
  • 4 Slices of cheese (Depending on taste desired)
  • 2 cans Tomato Soup (Optional)
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Line baking sheet with foil.
  3. Lightly butter 1 side of 4 of the slices of bread.
  4. Place bread, butter side down, on the baking sheet.
  5. Top each of the slices with 2 slices of turkey and 1 slice of cheese, followed by the remainin slices of bread.
  6. Butter the tops of the bread.
  7. Bake 10-12 minutes, turning once halfway through, until bread is golden brown.
  8. SLice in quarters and serve with tomato soup. (Optional)

Cresent Dogs with Dipping Sauce
  • 1 8oz. pkg. of Refridgerated Cresent Rolls
  • 8 Hot Dogs
  • 2-4 oz. Deli Cheddar Cheese Slices, Cut Into Strips
  • ½ Cup Canned RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies
  • ½ Cup Ranch Dressing
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Unroll dough and separate.
  3. Roll each portion into a 12" rope and wrap in a spiral around each hot dog.
  4. Arrange on a foil-lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Top with cheese.
  7. Bake an additional 1-2 minutes, until the cheese is melted.
  8. Combine RO*TEL and ranch dressing, then serve.

Tuna Sandwich To-Go
Children's Recipe
  • 2 Cans (5 oz. each) Water-Packed Tuna
  • 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 Apple, Chopped
  • 2 tbsp Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  • 1 Tomato, Sliced
  • 8 Slices Bread
  1. Combine tuna, cheese, apple, and mayonnaise in a medium bowl.
  2. For each sandwich, spread ¼ cup of the tuna mixtureon a slice of bread.
  3. On the other slice of bread, top with tomato.

Cheesy Chicken Quesadilla
  • 4 oz. Quick-Melting Cheese, Cubed
  • 1 Cup Cream Cheese
  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 3 tbsp Green Onions, Sliced
  • 12 oz. Cooked Chicken Breast, Diced
  • 8-8" Tortillas
  • ½ Cup Sour Cream
  1. In a small mixing bowl, mix together ½ cup of salsa with the cream cheese.
  2. Spread salsa mixture on 4 tortillas, then sprinkle with remaining ingredients, excluding salsa and sour cream.
  3. Cover with remaining tortillas.
  4. Spray a lg. skillet with cooking spray.
  5. Cook quesadillas on medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until crisp and lightly browned.
  6. Cut into wedges and garnish with sour cream and remaining salsa.

Bacon Cheeseburger Pizza
  • 1 Pizza Crust of Your Choosing
  • 1¼ Cups Pizza Sauce
  • 5 Slices Bacon, Crumbled
  • 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  • 12 oz. Quick-Melting Cheese, Cubed
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • ⅔ Cup Shredded "Fiesta Blend" Type Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Brown beef in skillet on medium heat; drain fat.
  3. Unroll the pizza crust onto greased baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce on crust.
  5. Top crust with remaining ingredients.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.

BBQ Beef Brisket Sandwiches w/Coleslaw
  • 1 Red Onion, Divided
  • 2 Cups Green Cabbage, Shredded
  • 2 Cups Red Cabbage, Shredded
  • ¼ Mayonnaise or Whipped Salad Dressing
  • 1 Bottle (18 oz.) BBQ Sauce, Divided
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Brisket (3 Lb.) Beef, Trimmed
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Beef Broth
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 12 Kaiser Rolls
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Cut ½ the onion into thin slices; place in a lg. bowl.
  3. Add cabbage; mix lightly.
  4. Mix dressing and 2 tbsp BBQ sauce.
  5. Add to cabbage mixture; toss to coat.
  6. Refridgerate until ready to use.
  7. Heat oill in a lg. skillet; add meat and cook for 5 minutes, or until browned on both sides; turn after 3 minutes.
  8. Transfer to a 13x9" baking dish.
  9. Chop remaining onion piece; sprinkle over meat.
  10. Mix broth, ½ cup of the remaining BBQ sauce, and garlic; pour over meat and cover.
  11. Bake for about 2½-3 hours or until meat is done.
  12. Let stand covered for about 10 minutes.
  13. Shred meat and place into a lg. bowl.
  14. Add remaining BBQ suace and toss to coat.
  15. Fill each roll with ½ cup meat mixture and ⅓ cup coleslaw just before serving.
