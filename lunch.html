Baked Turkey and Cheese Sandwiches
  • 1 tbsp Butter, Softened
  • 8 Slices of Bread (Depending on taste desired)
  • 8 Slices of Deli Turkey
  • 4 Slices of cheese (Depending on taste desired)
  • 2 cans Tomato Soup (Optional)
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Line baking sheet with foil.
  3. Lightly butter 1 side of 4 of the slices of bread.
  4. Place bread, butter side down, on the baking sheet.
  5. Top each of the slices with 2 slices of turkey and 1 slice of cheese, followed by the remainin slices of bread.
  6. Butter the tops of the bread.
  7. Bake 10-12 minutes, turning once halfway through, until bread is golden brown.
  8. SLice in quarters and serve with tomato soup. (Optional)

Cresent Dogs with Dipping Sauce
  • 1 8oz. Package of Refridgerated Cresent Rolls
  • 8 Hot Dogs
  • 2-4 oz. Deli Cheddar Cheese Slices, Cut Into Strips
  • ½ Cup Canned RO*TEL Diced Tomatoes and Green Chilies
  • ½ Cup Ranch Dressing
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Unroll dough and separate.
  3. Roll each portion into a 12" rope and wrap in a spiral around each hot dog.
  4. Arrange on a foil-lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Top with cheese.
  7. Bake an additional 1-2 minutes, until the cheese is melted.
  8. Combine RO*TEL and ranch dressing, then serve.

** UNKOWN RECIPE **
  • No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 2 Cans (15 oz. each)Chef Boyardee® Beef Ravioli®
  • 1 Cup Frozen Loose Cut Leaf Spinach
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Diced Tomatoes, Drained
  • 1½ tsp Dried Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Cup Cottage Cheese
  • ½ tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Cup Shredded Italian Blend Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Spray 8x8" glass baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Place spinach in microwave-safe bowl; cover with plastic wrap and microwave on HIGH 2 minutes; squeeze spinach dry.
  4. Combine ravioli, drained tomatoes and Italian seasoning in medium microwave-safe bowl; cover with plastic wrap and microwave on HIGH 3-4 minutes or until mixture is hot.
  5. Combine cottage cheese, spinach, and black pepper in small bowl.
  6. Place ½ of ravioli mixture in bottom of baking dish. Top with ½ of cheese mixture and ½ cup shredded cheese; repeat layers.
  7. Bake 15 minutes or until cheese is hot and bubbly.

** UNKOWN RECIPE **
  • 2 Cans (15 oz. each)Chef Boyardee® Beefaroni®
  • 1 Can (15 oz.) Whole Black Beans, Drained, Rinsed
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Diced Tomatoes, Drained
  • 1 Cup Frozen Whole Kernal Corn
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • ¼ Cup Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese
  • ½ Cup Broken Yellow Tortilla Chips
  1. Place Beefaroni®, drained tomatoes, beans, corn, and cumin in large saucepan; stir to combine.
  2. Bring to boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese and broken chips just before serving.
