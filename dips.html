Dips

Welcome to the dip section!

Please scroll down the page to see the delicious dips for various snacks at your party. You might also find some things to go along with these dips in the Super Bowl section.

Two Cheese Spinach Artichoke Dip
  • 1 Pkg. Frozen Chopped Spinach, Thawed and Drained
  • 1 Lb. Quick-Melting Cheese
  • 4 oz. Cream Cheese
  • 1 Can Artichoke Hearts, Drained and Chopped
  1. Combine all ingredients in a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Microwave on HIGH for 5 minutes, or until cheese is completely melted; stirring halfway through.

Buffalo Chicken Dip
  • 1 Pkg. (8 oz.) Cream Cheese
  • ½ Cup Ranch Dressing
  • ½ Cup Buffalo Wing Sauce
  • 2 Cans (12.5 oz. each) Chicken Breast, Drained
  • ½ Cup Blue Cheese or Mozzarella Cheese, Crumbled
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Place cream cheese into a deep baking dish; stir until smooth.
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  4. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until hot and bubbling.
  5. Serve with crackers or vegetables.

Chili Cheese Bean Dip
  • 1 Can Refried Beans
  • 1 Cup Ketchup
  • 1 Cup Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  • 1 Can Green Chilies, Chopped
  1. Combine all ingredients into a small saucepan.
  2. Heat, stirring, until cheese is melted.
  3. Serve warm

Deluxe Artichoke Dip
  • 1 Cup Fresh Bread Crumbs, Torn
  • 1 Cup Parmesan Cheese, Grated & Divided
  • 1 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 can (14 oz.) Artichoke Hearts, Drained & Chopped
  • ¼ Cup Fresh Parsley, Chopped
  • 1 Container (8 oz.) Sour Cream
  • 1 Jar (12 oz.) Marinated Artichoke Hearts, Drained & Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Finely Chopped
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Combine bread crumbs, ¼ cup cheese and parsley in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Combine mayonnaise, sour cream, remaining ¾ cup cheese, artichokes, and garlic in a medium bowl.
  4. Evenly spread artichoke mixture in a 9" pie plate; top with bread crumb mixture, then drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake uncovered for 35 minutes or until heated; serve with chips.

Spicy Peanut Dip
  • 1 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1 Bunch Scallions, Chopped
  • 1 Jalepeño, Chopped
  • 2 Lg. Cloves of Garlic, Chopped
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Juice & Zest From 2 Limes
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  1. Combine all ingedients in a food processor; blend until smooth.
If desired, add warm water to thin and adjust flavors to taste.

Not Just Salsa
  • Simply add whole or refried beans and chunks of pepper jack cheese to salsa.

  • Cooked and crumbled sausage or chorizo is a nice addition as well.

Pimento Cheese Kicker
  • 1 Lb. Extra-Sharp Cheddar
  • 1 Jar (7 oz.) Pimentos, Chopped
  • 1 tbsp Horseradish
  • 1 tsp Chili Powder
  • ½-⅔ Cup Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper, To Taste
  1. Finely grate cheese and place into a Lg. bowl.
  2. Gently stir in the pimentos, horseradish, chili powder, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stir until smooth, but DO NOT mash; mixture should have noticable bits of pimento.
  4. Refridgerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Cheese & Chili Dip
  • 1 Box (16 Oz) Quick-Melting Cheese
  • 1 Can (15 Oz) Chili, No Beans
  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine cheese and chili.
  2. Microwave on HIGH for 5 minutes, stirring after 3 minutes.
  3. Serve hot with chips, French bread chunks, or cornbread.
