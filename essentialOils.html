All-Purpose Cleaner

  • 2 tbsp Distilled White Vinegar

  2. Shake in spray bottle until borax is dissolved.

  4. Mixture will stay moist for a long time in a glass jar w/tight lid.

Homemade Laundry Soap
  • 1 bar of Soap, like Fels Naptha®, Ivory®, or Zote®
  • ½ - 1 Cup Washing Soda, like Arm & Hammer® Washing Soda
  • ½ - 1 Cup Borax, like 20 Mule Team®
    • NOTE: These items should be found in the laundry isle of your favorite store
  • 20 Drops Any Essential Oil
  • 6 Cups Hot Water
  1. Grate the soap into a saucepan.
  2. Cover with hot water and cook over medium-low heat stirring until soap is melted.
  3. Mix the washing soda and the borax in a 5-gallon bucket.
  4. Pour in the hot, melted soap mixture; stir until the powder is dissolved.
  5. Fill the bucket to near the top with more hot tap water.
  6. Stir, then cover and let set overnight to form a gel.
  7. The next day, stir.
  8. To use, put ½ cup of soap mixture and ½ cup of water per load
    9. You may mix equal parts of the water and the soap mixture in a smaller container; When ready to use, just pour 1 cup. Remember to shake well before each use.
