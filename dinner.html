Pepperoni Spaghetti Bake
  • No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 1¼ Cups Original Baking Mix
  • ½ Cup Milk
  • 2 Cans (14.5 oz. each) Chef Boyardee® Spaghetti & Meatballs
  • ¾ Cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, Divided
  • ⅓ Cup Sliced Pepperoni
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Spray 13x9" glass baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Combine baking mix and milk in a medium bowl; stir until soft dough forms
  4. Drop dough by spoonfuls on bottom of baking dish (will not completely cover the bottom).
  5. Spoon 1 can of spaghetti & meatballs over dough and sprinkle with ½ cup of cheese.
  6. Top with the remaining can of spaghetti & meatballs, remaining ¼ cup of cheese and pepperoni.
  7. Bake 20-30 minutes or until dough is done in centers and browned.

Cheeseburger-Roni
  • No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 2 Cans (15 oz. each)Chef Boyardee® Beefaroni®
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Diced Tomatoes, Drained
  • ¼ Cup Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Finely Chopped Dill Pickle
  • 1 tbsp Yellow Mustard
  • ¼ Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Hamburger Bun w/ Sesame Seeds, Torn Into Small Pieces
  1. Preheat broiler.
  2. Spray 10" oven-safe skillet with cooking spray; heat over med-high heat.
  3. Add Beefaroni®, drained tomatoes, ketchup, pickle, and mustard to skillet.
  4. Heat 5 minutes or until hot, stirring occasionally.
  5. Top with cheese.
  6. Lightly spray torn bun with cooking spray and place on top of cheese.
  7. Place skillet under broiler 2-3 minutes or until bun pieces are browned and crisp.

Ravioli Lasagna
  • No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 2 Cans (15 oz. each)Chef Boyardee® Beef Ravioli®
  • 1 Cup Frozen Loose Cut Leaf Spinach
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Diced Tomatoes, Drained
  • 1½ tsp Dried Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Cup Cottage Cheese
  • ½ tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Cup Shredded Italian Blend Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Spray 8x8" glass baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Place spinach in microwave-safe bowl; cover with plastic wrap and microwave on HIGH 2 minutes; squeeze spinach dry.
  4. Combine ravioli, drained tomatoes and Italian seasoning in medium microwave-safe bowl; cover with plastic wrap and microwave on HIGH 3-4 minutes or until mixture is hot.
  5. Combine cottage cheese, spinach, and black pepper in small bowl.
  6. Place ½ of ravioli mixture in bottom of baking dish. Top with ½ of cheese mixture and ½ cup shredded cheese; repeat layers.
  7. Bake 15 minutes or until cheese is hot and bubbly.

Taco-Roni
  • 2 Cans (15 oz. each)Chef Boyardee® Beefaroni®
  • 1 Can (15 oz.) Whole Black Beans, Drained, Rinsed
  • 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Diced Tomatoes, Drained
  • 1 Cup Frozen Whole Kernal Corn
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • ¼ Cup Shredded Mexican Blend Cheese
  • ½ Cup Broken Yellow Tortilla Chips
  1. Place Beefaroni®, drained tomatoes, beans, corn, and cumin in large saucepan; stir to combine.
  2. Bring to boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with cheese and broken chips just before serving.

Taco Joes
  • 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 Can (15.5 oz.) Manwich® Sloppy Joe Mix
  • ½ tsp Ground Cumin
  • 2 Cups Shredded Lettuce
  • 12 Taco Shells (Soft or Hard)
  • 1 Cup Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Cup Chopped Tomatoes
  1. In a large skillet, cook beef over medium-high heat for about 7 minutes or until crumbled, stirring occasionally.
  2. Stir in sloppy joe sauce and cumin.
  3. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Divide lettuce evenly between taco shells.
  5. Place about ¼ cup meat mixture in each taco shell, over lettuce.
  6. Top meat evenly with cheese and tomatoes.

Easy Manicotti
  • 1 Pkg. Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Pkg. Frozen Spinach, Chopped, Thawed, Squeezed Dry
  • 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese, Divided
  • ¼ Cup Grated Parmesan Chesse
  • 2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • ¼ tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Pkg. Dry Manicotti Shells, Uncooked
  • 1 Can Pasta Sauce, Divided
  • Non-Stick Cooking Spray
  • 1 Cup Water
  1. Prehaet oven to 375°.
  2. Stir together ricotta cheese, spinach, 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  3. Fill each uncooked shell with 3 tbsp of the mixture.
  4. Pour ½ the sauce into a sprayed 13x9" baking dish.
  5. Arrange filled shells in single layer over sauce; pour remaining sauce over the top.
  6. Pour water into corner of the dish.
  7. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  8. Cover tightly with aluminum foil sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
  9. Bake for about 1 hr and 15 minutes, or until pasta is tender.
  10. Let stand covered for about 10 minutes prior to serving.

Fettuccini Alfredo
  • 4 Cups Fettuccini Noodles
  • ¼ Cup Whipping Cream
  • ¾ Cup Parmesan Cheese, Freshly Grated
  • White Pepper
  • 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
  • ½ Lb. Soft Butter
  1. Cook Fettuccini in 4 qts. of water and 1 tsp of salt for about 14-18 minutes, then drain.
  2. In same pan add the butter and toss with the fettuccini noodles.
  3. Combine cream and egg yolk together.
  4. Alternate with ⅓ cream and ⅓ cheese.
  5. Toss well, but gently after each addition.
  6. Add pepper to taste and serve.

Double Quick Dinner Rolls
  • 2¼ tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 Cup Warm Water (110° F)
  • ¼ Cup Dry Potato Flakes
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • ¾ tsp Onion Powder
  • 2¼ Cups Flour
  1. In a Lg. bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water.
  2. Add potato flakes sugar, salt, egg, vegetable oil, onion powder, and 1 cup of the flour; beat until smooth.
  3. Stir in remaining flour; Continue stirring until smooth, scraping batter from the side of the bowl.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise in warm place until doubled in size. (About 20-30 minutes)
  5. Preheat over to 400°.
  6. Lightly grease 12 muffin cups; punch out dough.
  7. Spoon dough into 12 greased mufin cups, filling each one about ½ full.
  8. Let rise until batter reaches tops of the cups. (About 20 minutes)
  9. Bake for 15 minutes and serve immediately.

Cheesy Bean & Rice Csserole
Children's Recipe
  • 3 Cups Brown Rice, Cooked
  • 1 Can (16 Oz.) Kidney or Pinto Beans, Drained
  • 1 Lg. Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Cup Cottage Cheese
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 3 Oz. Cheddar Cheese, Grated
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In a Lg. mixing bowl, combine the rice, beans, onion, garlic, cottage cheese, and flour.
  3. Pour the mixture into a casserole dish; top with the grated cheese.
  4. Bake covered for 30 minutes and then un-covered for an additional 5-10 minutes until the cheese is golden brown.
For spicier flavor, stir in 2-4 tbsp of chopped green chili peppers before baking.

3x Mac & Cheese
  • 2 Cups Elbow-Style Macaroni Noodles - Un-cooked
  • 12 oz. Quick-Melting Cheese, Cubed
  • ⅓ Cup Milk
  • ⅔ Cup Fiesta-Style Shredded Cheese
  • ½ Cup Bread Crumbs
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In a pan with boiling water, cook the macaroni according to the package.
  3. Drain macaroni, then return to the pan.
  4. Add the quick-meltig cheese and the milk; stirring on low heat until the cheese is melted.
  5. Spread the mac & cheese mixture in a casserole dish and sprinkle with the shredded cheese.
  6. Top with bread crumbs; bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Backstage Burgers
  • 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 pkg. (1 Oz.) Ranch Seasoning
  • 2 tbsp Water
  • 2 Onions
  • 6 Kaiser Rolls
  • ½ Cup BBQ Sauce
  1. Light grill and heat until coals are red or gas grill is to temp.
  2. Meanwhile, combine the beef, ranch seasoning, water in a lg. bowl.
  3. Form the ixture into 6 patties.
  4. Slice the onions into ½" Slices.
  5. Place the burgers and the onions onto the grill.
  6. Grill for about 4 minutes, or until the meat is firm around the outside and the juices begin to pool.
  7. Flip burgers and onions; continue grilling for 3 minutes, or until done to your preference.
    (The internal temp should reach at least 160°)
  8. Spread BBQ sauce onto Kaiser rolls; place burgers and onions and any other desired toppings onto Kaiser rolls and enjoy.

Skillet Cheesy Chicken & Rice
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1¼ Lb. Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast Halves
  • 1 Can (10¾ Oz.) Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1½ Cup water
  • ½ tsp. Onion Powder
  • ¼ tsp. Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Cup White Rice, Uncooked
  • 2 Cups Mixed Vegetables, Frozen
  • ½ Cup Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
  1. Heat oil in 12" skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook for 10 minutes, or until well browned on both sides; remove chicken from skillet.
  3. Stir soup, water, onion powder, black pepper, and rice in skillet and heat to a boil; reduce heat to low.
  4. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, stirring once about halway through.
  5. Stir in the veggetables and return the chicken to the skillet; sprinkle with cheese.
  6. Cover and cook for about 5 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and rice is tender.

Orange Teriyaki Beef Roast
  • 1 Oven Bag, Large Size
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • ½ Cup Orange Juice
  • ½ Cup Teryiaki Sauce
  • ¼ Cup Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 tsp Orange Peel, Grated
  • ½ tsp Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Lb. Beef Top Round Roast, 1" Thick
  • Hot Cooked Rice (Optional)
  • ¼ Cup Green Onions, Sliced
  1. Shake flour in the bag; place into a 13x9x2" baking pan.
  2. Add orange juice, teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, garlic, orange peel, and crushed red pepper flakes to the bag.
  3. Squeeze bag to blend in flour.
  4. Add beef to the bag; turn the bag to coat the beef with the sauce.
  5. Close the bag with the nylon tie; marinate the beef in the refridgerator for 1½-2 hrs.
  6. Preheat oven to 325°.
  7. Cut six ½" slits in the top of the oven bag; tuck the ends of the bag into the pan.
  8. Bake for 30-45 minutes or until the meat thermometer reads 145°F. (DO NOT OVERCOOK)
  9. Thinly slice the beef.
  10. Stir the sauce and serve with rice, if desired.
  11. Sprinkle with chopped green onions.

Honey Dijon Chicken
  • 1 Oven Bag, Large Size
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 1 Can (10¾ Oz.) Condensed Cream of Chicken or Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 Jar (4.5 Oz.) Mushrooms, Sliced & Drained (Optional)
  • 2 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • ⅛ tsp Paprika
  • ⅛ tsp Garlic Powder
  • 4 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast Halves
  • Hot Cooked Rice
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Shake flour in oven bag; place bag into a 13x9x2" baking pan.
  3. Add soup, mushrooms, mustard, honey, paprika, and garlic powder to the bag.
  4. Squeeze bag to blend in flour; sprinkle chicken with additional paprika.
  5. Arrange the ingredients in an even layer inside of the bag.
  6. Close the bag with the nylon tie.
  7. Cut six ½" slits in the top of the oven bag; tuck the ends of the bag into the pan.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the meat thermometer reads 170°F.
  9. Serve ove rhot noodles or rice.

Baked Potato Casserole
  • 8 Medium Potatoes (about 2½-3 Lbs. Total),Peeled & Cut Into 1" Chunks
  • 1 Cup Evaperated Milk
  • ½ Cup Sour Cream
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • ½ tsp. Black Pepper
  • 2 Cups Cheddar Cheese, Shredded & Divided
  • 6 Slices Bacon, Cooked, Crumbled, & Divided
  • Sliced Green Onions (Optional)
  1. Place potatoes into a Lg saucepan; cover with water, then bring to a boil.
  2. Cook over medium-high heat for about 15-20 minutes, or until tender; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°; grease 2½-3 Qt. casserole dish.
  4. Return the potatoes to the saucepan; add evaperated milk, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
  5. Beat with hand-held mixer until smooth.
  6. Stir in 1½ cups of cheese and ½ of the bacon.
  7. Spoon the mixture into the casserole dish.
  8. Bake for about 20-25 minutes or until heated through; top with the remaining ½ cup of cheese, remaining bacon, and green onions.
  9. Continue baking for about 3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted.
