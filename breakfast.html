Stuffed Cream Cheese Pancakes w/Applesauce Topping
  • 1 Pgk. Cream Cheese
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1½ Cups Applesauce, Room Temp
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Baking Soda
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 1½ Cups Buttermilk, Room Temp
  • 3 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Lg. Egg
  1. Preheat oven to 175°.
  2. In a lg. bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk the buttermilk, oil, and egg.
  4. Stir into the dry ingredients; the batter should be slightly lumpy.
  5. In a mixer, mix cream cheese and honey together; set aside.
  6. Heat a non-stick griddle over medium heat; scoop about ¼ cup of the batter for each pancake on top of the griddle; allowing plenty of space for spreading.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface (1-3 minutes); turn and cook until golden brown (1-2 minutes).
  8. Keep cooked pancakes in oven to keep warm while you cook remaining pancakes.
  9. When ready to serve, take 6 pancakes and spread the filling on each; top with another pancake, and then top with applesauce (Optional)

French Toast Pockets w/Creamy Caramel Syrup
For the French toast pockets:

  • 8 Thick Slices Bread
  • 8 Oz. Cream Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Vanilla
  • 6 tbsp Sugar, Divided
  • ½ Cup Pecans, Chopped
  • 7 Lg. Eggs
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • ½ tsp Nutmeg
  • 1 Cup Corn Flakes
  • ½ Cup Coconut
  • 1
For the caramel syrup:

  • 2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 2 Cups Light Karo® Syrup
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar, Packed
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1
  • 1
  1. Combine cream cheese, vanilla, pecans, and 2 tbsp of sugar; set aside.
  2. Combine eggs, milk, nutmeg, and 4 tbsp of sugar, until well blended; set aside.
  3. Combine corn flakes with coconut; set aside.
  4. Cut down through the top and along one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Spread with a generous tbsp of cream cheese filling inside of each pocket.
  6. Preheat a lightly greased griddle to 325°.
  7. Dip bread pockets into egg/milk mixture, then into corn flake/coconut mixture.
  8. Coat both sides of each pocket slice.
  9. Cook until golden on both sides and each slice is warmed through; serve with creamy caramel syrup.

For the caramel syrup:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan, stir over low heat until smooth.
  2. Bring to a folling boil and maintain for about 5 minutes; Continue to stir.
  3. Remove from heat and serve warm

Cheesy Breakfast Biscuit Sandwich
  • 1 Tube Biscuits
  • 8 Slices Ham, Bite-Sized
  • 8 oz. Quick-Melting Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Place the biscuits on an ungreased baking sheet.
  3. Bake for about 13-17 minutes, or until golden brown.
  4. Open each biscuit and place ham and cheese on biscuit.
  5. Put biscuit back together, then place into the microwave for about 30-60 seconds.

Healthy Banana Bran Muffin
Children's Recipe
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ Cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg, Beaten
  • 1 Medium-Ripe Banana, Mashed
  • ¾ Cup Milk
  • 2 Cups Bran Flake Cereal
  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. In a lg. bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and sugar; set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix egg, banana, milk, and cereal.
  4. Let stand for 5 minutes, and then stir mixture until cereal is completely mashed.
  5. Add cereal and milk mixture to flour mixture; stir until well blended.
  6. Spoon batter into a greased muffin pan and fill to ⅔ full.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown; serve warm.
