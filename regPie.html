Post® Crunchy Pecan-Apple Pie
  • 3 tbs. Butter, Divided
  • 3 Granny Smith Apples, Peeled, Cut Into Slices
  • 2 Golden Delicious Apples, Peeled, Cut Into Slices
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 4 Cups Post® Selects Great Grains-Raisins, Date, & Pecan Whole Grain Cereal, Divided
  • 1 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Deep Dish Pie Crust, 9", Thawed or Fresh
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Melt 1 tbs. of the butter in a lg. skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add apples; toss to coat.
  4. Cover with lid; cook for 6-8 minutes, or until apples are tender; stirring frequently.
  5. Add 2 cups of the cereal, the sugar and the cinnamon; mix lightly.
  6. Spoon into the pie crust; level top with back of spoon.
  7. Cover with remaining 2 cups of cereal.
  8. Melt remaining 2 tbs. butter; drizzle over cereal.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until crust and top of pie is lightly brown, covering loosely with foil after 30 minutes.

Oreo® Pudding Pie
  • 2 Pkgs. Cookie 'N Cream Instant Pudding
  • 2½ Cups Cold Milk
  • 1 Tub (8 Oz.) Whipped Topping, Thawed & Divided
  • 1 Oreo®Pie Crust
  • 5 Oreo® Cookies, Cut In ½
  1. Beat pudding mixes and milk in a Lg. bowl with a whisk for about 2 minutes.
  2. Sir in ½ of the whipped topping (4 Oz.).
  3. Pour the mixture into the pie crust.
  4. Refridgerate for 4 hours, or until firm.
  5. Top with remaining whipped topping and cookies just before serving; keep refridgerated.

Oreo® Ice Cream Shoppe Pie
  • 2 Pkgs. Cookie 'N Cream Instant Pudding
  • 1¼ Cups Cold Milk
  • 1 Tub (8 Oz.) Whipped Topping, Thawed & Divided
  • 1 Oreo®Pie Crust
  • ½ Cup Hot Fudge Ice Cream Topping, Divided
  1. Reserve 2 Tbsp. of the fudge topping; spread remaining onto the bottom of the crust.
  2. Cover with ½ of the whipped topping; freeze for 10 minutes.
  3. Beat pudding mixes and milk in a Lg. bowl with a whisk for about 2 minutes. (Mixture will be thick)
  4. Stir in remaining whipped topping; spoon over the other whipped topping layer in crust.
  5. Freeze for about 4 hours, or until firm; remove from the freezer for about 15 minutes before serving.
  6. Drizzle with desired amount of fudge topping; store leftover pie in the freezer.
