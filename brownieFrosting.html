Chocolate Brownie Frosting
(Makes about 1⅔ Cups)
  • 6 tbsp Butter, Room Temp
  • 6 tbsp Cocoa
  • 1 tbsp Light Corn Syrup or Honey
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Cups Powdered Sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp milk
  1. Beat butter, cocoa, corn syrup (or honey) in small bowl until blended.
  2. Add powdered sugar and milk; beat to spreading consistancy

Raspberry Brownie Sauce
(Makes about 12 servings)
  • 1 pt Fresh Raspberries
  • 2 tbsp Powdered Sugar
  • 3 drops Lemon Juice
  • ½ tsp Sea Salt
  1. Place raspberries in bowl.
  2. Sift powdered sugar on top of raspberries.
  3. Add lemon juice and salt.
  4. pour mixture into blender and purée
  5. Strain and serve.
  6. Drizzle over brownies.
