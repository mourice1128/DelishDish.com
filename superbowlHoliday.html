Christmas Chocolate Peppermint Graham
  • 11 oz Milk Chocolate, Melted
  • 11 oz White Chocolate, Melted
  • 4 Peppermint Candy Canes, Crushed
  • 24 Graham Squares
  1. Dip one end of the graham into milk chocolate, lay on parchment paper.
  2. Repeat with all the grahams.
  3. Allow to set in the fridge for about 15 minutes.
  4. Dip the other end into the white chocolate; sprinkle with candy cane pieces immediately after.
  5. Allow to set.

Picante-Glazed Chicken Wings
  • 12 Chicken Wings (About 2 Lbs.)
  • 1 Jar (16 Oz.) Pace® Picante Sauce
  • ⅓ Cup Orange Marmalade
  • 2 tsp Dijon-Style Mustard
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seed, Toasted
  1. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. Line rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Cut off the chicken wing tips and discard.
  4. Cut the chicken wings in ½ at the joint.
  5. Heat 1½ cups picante sauce, marmalade, and mustard in a 2 qt. saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to medium-low.
  7. Cook for 40 minutes or until the mixture is reduced to ¾, stirring ocasionally.
  8. Place the chicken into a Lg. bowl; add the remaining picante sauce and toss to coat.
  9. Place the chicken onto the baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 40 minutes or util the chicken is cooked through, turning the chicken over once halfway through the bake time.
  11. Batse the chicken with the picante-mustard mixture during the last 10 minutes of the bake time.
  12. Sprinkle with the sesame seed.

Game Day Chili
  • 1 Slow Cooker Liner
  • 1 Lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Green Bell Pepper, Chopped
  • 1 Small Onion, Chopped
  • 2 Cans (15 Oz. ea) Red Kidney Beans, Undrained
  • 2 Pkgs Chili Seasoning
  • 1 Can (15 Oz.) Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Can (10 Oz.) Tomatoes, Diced
  • 1 Can Green Chilies
  • Cheese & Sour Cream (For Garnishment)
  1. Place the slow cooker liner snugly in the slow cooker bowl and stretch top of the liner over the rim of the bowl.
  2. Brown the ground beef, bell pepper, and onion over medium-high heat in a Lg skillet; drain well.
  3. Add beef mixture into the slow cooker liner.
  4. In a Lg bowl, combine the remaining ingredients, then add to the beef; stirring gently after.
  5. Place lid on the slow cooker and cook on LOW for 6-7 hours OR on HIGH for 3-3½ hours or until heated through.
  6. Serve directly from the slow cooker.
