THANKSGIVING TREATS

Pumpkin Pie
  • 1 Cup Pumpkin Pie Filling
  • ½ Cup Bisquick® Mix
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 1 Can Evaporated Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Soft Butter
  • 1½ tsp Pumpkin Spice
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Eggs
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Use broiler - Fill bottom with water.
  2. Place pumpkin on top part, cook 20 minutes.
  3. Stir ingredients in large bowl with wisk until well blended (35-40 minutes)
  4. Cool in fridge for 2 hours.

CHRISTMAS TREATS

Holiday Present Treats
  • 3 tbs. Butter
  • 1 Pkg. (10 Oz.) Marshmallows
  • 6 Cups Crispy Rice Cereal
  • 6 Cups Cocoa Crispy Rice Cereal
  • Prepared Frosting or Decorating Gel
  • Assorted Candies
  • Red string Licorice
  1. Melt butter in Lg. saucepan over low heat.
  2. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted; remove from heat.
  3. Add cereals, stirring until well coated.
  4. Using a buttered spatula or wax paper, evenly press into a 13"x9"x2" greased pan.
  5. Cool; cut into 2" squares.
  6. Use frosting to fasten stacks of 2 squares together.
    Decorate with additional frosting and/or candies.
    Tie bows on packages with the licorice.
    Best if served the same day.

Reindeer Snacks
  • 3 Cups Butter Popcorn, Popped
  • 3 Cups Bugles® Corn Snacks
  • 2 Cups Pretzel Sticks
  • 1 Cup Toasted O Cereal
  • ⅓ Cup Butter, Melted
  • ½ tsp. Chili Powder
  • ¼ tsp. Garlic Powder
  1. Heat over to 300°.
  2. In Lg bowl, mix popcorn, snacks, pretzels, and cereal.
  3. In a small bowl, mix remaining ingredients.
  4. Drizzle over the popcorn mixture, tossing until evenly coated.
  5. Spread into an un-greased 15"x10"x1" pan.
  6. Bake uncovered for 15 minutes, stirring twice.
  7. Cool; store loosly covered at room temp for up to 2 weeks
